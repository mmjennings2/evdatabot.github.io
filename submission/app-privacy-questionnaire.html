App Privacy Questionnaire

ASC section: App Privacy. This is a click-through questionnaire, not a paste field. Use the table below to answer each question. The answers MUST match the privacy manifest in the iOS app exactly — Apple's automated comparator can pull the app post-acceptance if there's a mismatch.

Top-level question

Q: Do you or your third-party partners collect data from this app?
A: Yes — Apple's definition of "collect" includes "the app reads the data on-device" even if it's never transmitted. EVDataBot reads CLLocation, so this answer is Yes.

Q: Tracking?
A: No — NSPrivacyTracking=false in both privacy manifests.

Why "Location — Precise" is Yes

Apple's questionnaire treats "the app reads precise location" as "collected" regardless of whether the data leaves the device. EVDataBot reads CLLocation for trip route recording — answering "No" because data stays on-device would be a mismatch that Apple's automated comparator flags.

Submission checklist before saving

  • Re-read this table against the live privacy policy right before submission — verify Location is the only "collected" data type.
  • Confirm the manifest cross-reference in the iOS app at EVDataBot/Resources/PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy declares precisely the same single data type.
  • App Privacy Contact email is set per the App Privacy Contact block.
